Hlif Thompson

1900 - 1969

On Tuesday, October 21st, 1969 at the Portage la Prairie General Hospital, Mrs. Hlif Thompson, beloved wife of Helgi Thompson of Langruth, Man.

Mrs. Thompson was born December 1st, 1900 in Selkirk, the daughter of Mrs. Johnson and the late Mr. D. Johnson. She received her education in Lundar and Winnipeg schools, later to teach for a number of years. In 1928 she married Helgi Thompson and had resided in Langruth since that time.

Mrs. Thompson was a member of Grace Lutheran Church, Langruth and a Lutheran Ladies Aid, being a past president of the group.

She is survived by her husband Helgi, seven daughters, Mrs. K. (Margaret) Munro, Mrs. M. (Eileen) Nesbitt, Mrs. N. (Eleanor) Jonatchik, Mrs. R. (May) Ryplaniski, all of Winnipeg, Mrs. L. (Betty) Buthelet of Sturgeon Falls, Ontario, Mrs. B. (Linda) Hoffltron of Dryden, Ontario, Mrs. C. (Sylvia) Smith of Crystal City, Man., four sons, Stanley of Saskatoon, Sask., Harold of Winnipeg, Allan of Selkirk, Robert of Langruth and 25 grandchildren. Also surviving is her mother, Mrs. D. Johnson, two sisters, Pauline and Rosa, two brothers, Ragnar and Hjalti, all of Lundar. Mrs. Thompson was predeceased by a son, Clifford in 1946.

Funeral service will be held Friday, October 24th at 2 p.m. in Grace Lutheran Church, Langruth. Interment will follow in Big Point Cemetery. The Trimble Funeral Chapel in care of arrangements.

As published in The Winnipeg Tribune, 1969-10-22 ( Page 39) on 5 November 2022
